ARTE Museum

ARTE MUSEUM’ is built on a grand venue with the size of 4,500sqm, the highest height of 10-meters, after renovating the site where it used to be a speaker manufacturing factory. Under the theme of ‘ETERNAL NATURE’, ten different subjects and spaces are introduced through different media technologies in the exhibition zones; FLOWER, GARDEN, BEACH, WATERFALL, WORMHOLE, STAR, WAVE, NIGHT SAFARI, MOON, JUNGLE. You may also be able to experience ‘ARTE TEA BAR’ where media art meets F&B. Every exhibition halls are designed to provide not only the impressive visual experiences, also different mixture of sensuous sounds and fragrances suitable for each exhibition zones that immerse you into a new experience stimulating your sense of visual, hearing and smell..
